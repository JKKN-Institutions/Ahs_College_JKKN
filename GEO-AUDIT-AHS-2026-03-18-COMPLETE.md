## KEY GAPS: WHAT COMPETITORS HAVE THAT JKKN LACKS

### 1. **Community Q&A Presence (Reddit/Quora)**
- **Competitors:** PSG, SRM, SRIHER appear in 5+ Quora threads
- **JKKN:** Zero Quora answers, zero Reddit threads
- **Impact:** Students researching colleges never see JKKN in community discussions
- **Fix:** Create Quora answers, seed Reddit posts, encourage alumni reviews

### 2. **Program-Specific Visibility (Course Pages)**
- **Competitors:** Top 5 results for "B.Sc cardiac technology" include SRM, Amrita, JAIN, Christian
- **JKKN:** Zero mentions in course-specific searches despite offering same programs
- **Impact:** Students searching for specific programs bypass JKKN
- **Fix:** Create program-level landing pages with course names in H1 + meta title

### 3. **AI-Generated Content Mentions (GEO)**
- **Competitors:** None actively optimized for AI, but SRIHER/Saveetha appear in general medical searches
- **JKKN:** Present in 2/10 queries only (niche visibility)
- **Impact:** ChatGPT/Gemini users don't see JKKN in AI search results
- **Fix:** Add E-E-A-T signals (faculty profiles, research, certifications), implement Speakable schema

### 4. **Article/Blog Schema (AEO)**
- **Competitors:** None have Article schema (gap across industry)
- **JKKN:** No Article schema on blog posts
- **Impact:** Blog content not optimized for featured snippets or AI overviews
- **Fix:** Add Article schema to all blog posts (author, publication date, word count)

### 5. **Speakable Schema (Voice Search)**
- **Competitors:** None implemented
- **JKKN:** Not implemented
- **Impact:** Voice search users (Google Assistant, Alexa) don't surface JKKN content
- **Fix:** Add Speakable schema to FAQ answers (optional but high-value for smart speakers)

### 6. **Explicit Meta Robots Configuration**
- **Competitors:** None have explicit directives
- **JKKN:** Not specified
- **Impact:** Potential for accidental noindex/nofollow crawl issues
- **Fix:** Add explicit `<meta name="robots" content="index, follow, max-snippet:-1">`

### 7. **Geographic & Entity Disambiguation**
- **Competitors:** SRIHER (Chennai), PSG (Coimbatore), SRM (multiple campuses) have clear location strategy
- **JKKN:** Only appears in Salem-area search ("allied health science college near Salem") weakly
- **Impact:** Missing from geographic AI intent signals
- **Fix:** Optimize for "allied health science college Namakkal" + "allied health science Komarapalayam"
